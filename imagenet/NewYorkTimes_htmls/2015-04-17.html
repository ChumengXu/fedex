This week's cover story begins with an eye-popping statistic: More British Muslims have joined Islamic militant groups in Iraq, Syria and elsewhere than serve in Britain's armed forces. Mary Anne Weaver brings us into the world of the researchers who are trying to understand why that is, and who spent hours Skyping and texting with young recruits of ISIS and other organizations in search of the answer.
Also in print and online this week, Jonah Weiner introduces us to Brent White, the film editor who gives shape to the improvisational comedy of directors like Judd Apatow and Paul Feig; the photographer Sally Mann revisits the controversy surrounding nude pictures she took of her children two decades ago; Teju Cole considers remixed photography; and Mark Bittman savors the reinvented breakfasts of the Los Angeles chef Jessica Koslow.
Happy reading,
Jake Silverstein
